Why Donate

Blood Donation Saves Lives

One donation can save up to 3 lives. Blood cannot be manufactured — it can only come from generous donors like you.

💊

Safe & Simple

The donation process takes only 8–10 minutes. Your blood volume replenishes naturally within 24 hours.

🏥

Critical Need

Hospitals need a constant supply of blood for surgeries, emergencies, and patients with chronic conditions.

❤️

Save 3 Lives

Each whole blood donation can be separated into red cells, platelets, and plasma — helping multiple patients.

🩺

Free Health Check

Donors receive a complimentary mini health screening including blood pressure, hemoglobin, and pulse checks.
